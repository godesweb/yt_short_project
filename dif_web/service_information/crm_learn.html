CRM Development FAQs

How is custom CRM better than off-the-shelf solutions?

Custom CRM systems are built specifically for your business processes, eliminating the need to adapt your workflow to software limitations. They offer better scalability, ownership of data, and long-term cost savings compared to subscription-based solutions that may charge per user.

What technologies do you use for CRM development?

We use modern tech stacks including Laravel/Symfony for backend, React/Vue.js for frontend, MySQL/PostgreSQL for databases, and cloud platforms like AWS/Azure. For mobile CRM access, we develop native iOS/Android apps or progressive web apps based on your needs.

Can you integrate with our existing tools?

Absolutely. We regularly integrate with accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero), marketing tools (Mailchimp, HubSpot), communication platforms (Slack, Teams), and productivity tools. Our CRM solutions include API development for seamless connectivity with your existing tech stack.

What about data migration from our current system?

We provide complete data migration services, securely transferring your existing customer data, sales records, and historical information to your new CRM. Our process includes data cleaning, deduplication, and validation to ensure data integrity.
