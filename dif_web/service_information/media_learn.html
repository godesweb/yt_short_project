Social Media FAQs

How long before we see results?

Organic growth typically shows measurable improvement in 3-6 months. Paid campaigns can deliver immediate traffic/leads, while building genuine engagement takes consistent effort. We provide monthly progress reports showing key metrics.

Do we need to provide content?

We handle all content creation including writing, design, and scheduling. We may request brand assets (logos, product photos) or collaborate on content ideas, but our team manages the entire process.

How do you measure success?

We track platform-specific metrics (engagement rate, reach) plus business outcomes (website traffic, leads generated). Every campaign includes clear KPIs tied to your goals, with transparent reporting.

What's your ad management fee?

We charge 15-20% of ad spend for campaign management (minimum $200/month). This includes strategy, creative, targeting, optimization, and reporting - everything except the actual platform ad costs.
