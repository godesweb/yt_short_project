Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop an app?

Development time varies based on complexity. Simple apps take 2-3 months, medium complexity 3-6 months, and complex enterprise solutions 6+ months. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Should I build native or cross-platform apps?

Native apps offer best performance and access to device features, while cross-platform solutions reduce cost and development time. We'll recommend the best approach based on your requirements, budget, and timeline.

Do you handle app store submissions?

Yes, we handle the complete app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including preparing all required assets, metadata, and compliance requirements.

What about ongoing maintenance?

We offer flexible maintenance plans that include bug fixes, OS updates, feature enhancements, and performance monitoring. Most clients opt for our monthly maintenance packages after launch.
