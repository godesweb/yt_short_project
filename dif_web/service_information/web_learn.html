Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the project complexity. A basic website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex sites or e-commerce solutions may take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide website hosting?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions and include 1 year of free hosting with all our website packages. After the first year, you can continue with our hosting or migrate to any provider of your choice.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely! We build most business websites with content management systems (like WordPress) that allow easy updates. We'll also provide training on how to manage your content.

What about after the website launches?

We offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, backups, content updates, and technical support. Many clients choose to retain us for regular website improvements and marketing services.
